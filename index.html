My UX Skills

User Research

To get to the heart of a user, I incorporate qualitative research (interviews) as well as quantitative (surveys and testing). Carefully crafting interview scripts and surveys based on proto-personas and user input is important to my design journey.

User Empathy

Since my goal is to create a positive and effective user experience, I set my point of view aside and immerse myself in all aspects of the user research through effective communication and attentiveness.

Qualitative Interviews

Initiating a comfortable setting to learn more about users is important to gain insight while hosting user interviews. I come up with three strong goals for information that will aid in the design process and base my questions off of them. This preparation allows me to be fully present to understand the user.
